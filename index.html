Program Relationship Health
Signals from session completion, reflection depth, and pacing. Hover any metric to see how it's measured.
Session consistency?
How this is measured
Percentage of scheduled sessions completed across all triads. Gaps over 10 days are flagged automatically. A missed session resets a triad's streak — the platform surfaces this before momentum is lost.
88%
Reflection depth?
How this is measured
Average word count and question completion rate across all post-session reflections. Triads that write more tend to engage more in subsequent sessions — this is the strongest single predictor of long-term program success.
74%
Teen confidence arc?
How this is measured
Two signals: how often the teen uses the "slow down" feature (protective pacing = growing judgment), and whether phase-advance timing speeds up over sessions. Teens who start cautious and become more decisive are developing real facilitation instinct.
81%
Cross-gen participation?
How this is measured
Whether all three generations contribute each session, measured via readiness signal timing. If one person signals ready consistently first and another consistently last, the triad may have a participation imbalance worth a facilitation note.
92%
Session engagement?
How this is measured
A composite of three lagging signals: phase pacing (healthy dwell time vs. rushing or stalling), readiness convergence (how quickly all three confirm), and prior reflection depth. Updated after each completed session — not live surveillance.

Partner onboarding
Welcome to 3G.
You're ready to begin.
The platform runs facilitation so you can focus on your people. Set up your cohort in four steps — after that, your first triad can run Session 1 without you in the room.
8 sessions per cohort
5 phases per session
~60 min per session
Setup checklist
Four things before Session 1
0
of 4
01
Add participants
Recruit one teen (14–17), one senior (65+), and one child (6–8) for each triad. You can add participants individually and match them in the next step.
02
Create a triad
Match one teen, one senior, and one child. Each triad runs its own 8-session arc — sessions, health scores, and reflections are tracked independently.
03
Set a schedule
Weekly or biweekly, in-person or virtual. Each triad can run on its own cadence — the platform tracks each one separately.
04
Share session links
Each participant gets a personal link — teen, senior, or child view. For in-person sessions, one shared device at the table runs the session screen.
What the platform handles for you
🎯
Session facilitation
Each session follows a structured 5-phase arc. Prompts, transitions, and timing are built in — no facilitator needed in the room.
📊
Health & continuity tracking
The platform monitors session consistency, reflection quality, and relationship depth across every triad — and surfaces risks before they become problems.
💬
Reflection & memory
After each session, the teen and senior reflect together. Their responses shape the next session and build a shared history over the full cohort.
How a session works — the 5-phase arc
1
Arrival
5 min
Settle in. The platform opens the session and introduces the day's theme.
2
Connection
10 min
A warm-up prompt brings the three generations into conversation before the activity.
3
Activity
25 min
The core of the session. Structured, generation-specific roles that create real interaction.
4
Reflection
15 min
Teen and senior reflect together. Responses are saved and shape the next session.
5
Close
5 min
A brief ritual to end the session. Builds continuity and a sense of shared rhythm.
Session formats
🏠
In-person
One shared device at the table. The platform guides the flow — participants just follow along. No facilitator required once the session starts.
💻
Virtual
Each participant joins from their own device with a personalized view. Activities and timing sync automatically across all three screens.
Who handles what
Your role
  • Recruit teen, senior, and child participants
  • Create triads and set schedules
  • Share session links with each participant
  • Monitor triad health from this dashboard
  • Reach out to 3G if a triad needs support
Platform handles
  • All in-session facilitation and transitions
  • Session arc across all 8 sessions
  • Reflection capture between sessions
  • Health scoring and risk flagging
  • Personalized views for each generation
